Visual System

Links and Literature

On the Web

  • SensesWeb Chapter 1 & 2 contain a very good introduction the the Eye and the Primary Visual Cortex.
  • Webvision - The Organization of the Retina and Visual System Probably the best page on the visual system. Very up-to-date.
  • Optical Illusions A fantastic collection of examples of optical illusions (by Michael Bach). You HAVE to check it out!
  • The Lena Story How the most frequently used picture in image processing got into the field.
  • Kimball's Biology Pages, an online textbook for biology that has won a number of awards. These pages also contain interesting articles on the human eye, and to the insect eye.
  • Eye Design Book contains pretty much everything about eyes: optical principles, spider eyes, human eyes, CCD sensors, .... (I want to point out, though, that I am very much opposed to Curt Decker's "Intelligent Design" believes!)
  • The Joy of Visual Perception: A Web Book (by Peter K. Kaiser) Somewhat less technical than the "Eye Design Book", nice to browse through.
  • A collection of Links on Signal and Image Processing.

Reading

Reviews on Retinal Implants

  • Stemcells, Optogenetics, and Retinal Prosthetics (Wood et al., Retina 2019)
  • Current overview article on existing retinal implants (Nowik et al., J Clinical Neurosci 2020)
  • An update on retinal prostheses (Ayton et al, Clinical Neurophysiology 2020)
  • Advances in retinal prosthesis systems (Bloch et al., Therapeutic Advances in Ophthalmology 2019)

Other interesting articles

  • Wide-field approach by EPFL researchers (2021). See also "Photovoltaic retinal prosthesis resores high-resolution responses to single-pixel stimulation in blind retinas" (Chenais et all, CommMaterials 2021)
  • Biologically-inspired image processing in computational retina models (Melanitis et al, J Comp Biol Med 2019)
  • The Neuronal Organization of the Retina (RH Masland, Neuron, 2012) Almost a must-read, on our current understanding of retinal image processing.
  • Computational Models of Early Human Vision (L. K. Cormack, from: Handbook of Image and Video Processing by A. Bovik, 1st edition, 2000) Good article on the simulation of neural activity in the retina and in the primary visual cortex. Note that this whole book is available at the ETH Online library, and that a new version of the book has appeared since.
  • Neuromorphic Sensory Systems (Liu and Delbruck, Current Opinion in Neurobiology 2010, 20: 1-8 A good introduction to the ideas of "neuromorphic computing" and "spike-based computing".
  • Optogenetics: Controling the Brain With Light (K. Deisseroth, SciAm Oct 2010) Optogenetics is arguably the biggest new technique in Neuroscience in the last decade.
